About Glass City Recovery Cafe

I created this space because recovery isn’t just about staying sober — it’s about rebuilding, reconnecting, and learning how to live again. As someone in recovery myself, I know how important it is to feel seen, supported, and not judged. Glass City Recovery Cafe is being built to bring people together, offer support, and create real connection in our community.

Your support helps create a safe space for recovery, connection, and second chances.
